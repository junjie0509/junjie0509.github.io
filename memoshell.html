A light and sound installation for distant bonding.

With a user-centered design approach, dynamic lighting and audio installation were developed to help grandparents promote bonding with their grandchildren from far away. The Memoshell and related services are designed to store voice clips for children it aims to promote bonding regardless of distance or time differences.

Role

Product Design

Date

2011.1-2011.6

Website

signify.com

In this 6 month internship, I have been working on how to promote long-term and intimate bonding between child and distant grandparents through intelligent lighting innovation.

Case Study

User Insights

  • Distant grandparents find it hard to bond with their grandchildren over distance and cultural differences.

Design Goals

  • Use advanced lighting technology to narrow the culture and distance gap.

A design process from user research to ideation, prototyping and concept validation.

Design for emotions.

Bonding is a vague concept, hard to measure and design, to carry it out needs throughout user research, walks in user's shoes, use my knowledge to bridge technology and user needs.
